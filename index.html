About Dr. Aijaz Ahmed

Medical Excellence & Patient Care

Professional Background

Dr. Aijaz Ahmed is a highly qualified General Physician with over 10 years of medical experience. He specializes in the management and treatment of chronic diseases including Diabetes, Blood Pressure disorders, and Kidney-related conditions.

Qualifications

  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)
  • Senior Registrar
  • PMDC Verified Professional

Our Approach

Dr. Aijaz believes in providing comprehensive, patient-centered care with a focus on prevention and long-term wellness. He takes time to understand each patient's unique health needs and develops personalized treatment plans.

Areas of Specialization
